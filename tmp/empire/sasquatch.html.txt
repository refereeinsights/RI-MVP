SASQUATCH 5-A-SIDE
Sasquatch 5 A Side is an Inland Northwest staple in our soccer community that hosts 100+ teams from the U5-adult levels in Spokane, WA at Spokane Falls Community College.
We invite teams from the Girls Academy, MLS Next, Elite 64, EA, USYS National League, ECNL, ECNL RL, National Premier Leagues, Washington Youth Soccer, and local recreational youth and adult leagues. Make sure to register soon; the tournament will sell out.  
June 1, 2025
 
Games held at: 
SPOKANE FALLS COMMUNITY COLLEGE
3410 W Whistalks Way
Spokane 
Early bird registration by April 10: $200/team
Regular registration after April 10: $250/team

TOURNAMENT INFORMATION
AGE – U5 (2021) - Adult
Fee: $250
Registration Deadline – May 16th
Location: Spokane Falls Community College
5-A-Side is open to teams from U5-Adult, Boys, Girls, & Adult Recreational, Select, & Premier
All teams are guaranteed 3 games!
No roster restrictions beyond gender/age.
Players may only play for one team in the same age group but can play for two different age group.
Games will consist of one single 35’ period with subbing on the fly.
Play ups are allowed.
