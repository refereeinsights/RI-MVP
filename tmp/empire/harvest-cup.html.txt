TOURNAMENT FORMAT
AGE BRACKETS - 2025-26 SEASON
Adult Coed - 2010 & Older
Girls 2012/2013
Girls 2016-2018
Boys High School – 2011-2007
Boys 2012
Boys 2014
Boys 2015/2016
5 v 5 (4 field players + Keeper)
Roster Size: 10 player suggested maximum
3 games guaranteed
Halves: 20-min (5-min half-time)
Field size: ~30 x 40 yds; goals 6 x 12 feet
Trophies for 1st place only
*Players added to roster after start of play may not exceed a maximum of 10 players on the roster (players may not be removed from the roster to make space for a new player)
A photo I.D. will be required by all adult players in case a question about the team’s roster arises.
All juniors and players 18 and older must sign the team waiver
